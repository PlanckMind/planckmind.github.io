About Me

Hello! I'm Nitish Rathore, a content creator and educator with a mission to make learning accessible, enjoyable, and transformative. Through my PlanckMind platform, I explore the intersection of productivity, technology, and consciousness, helping people unlock their potential and live more intentional lives.

The concept of PlanckMind represents the smallest possible unit of consciousness - the fundamental building block of awareness. Just as Planck length is the smallest measurable unit in physics, PlanckMind represents the irreducible element of conscious thought.

With a background in education and technology, I bring a unique perspective to content creation. My journey began as a teacher, evolved into content creation, and has now grown into a multi-platform educational ecosystem reaching thousands of learners worldwide.

For business inquiries and collaborations: PlanckMind@gmail.com
